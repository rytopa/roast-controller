Web-Bluetooth controller for Hamid / Matchbox (TC4) roasters — no vendor app, no WeChat.
Web Bluetooth isn't available. Use Chrome or Edge on Android or Windows/macOS/Linux, Bluetooth on. iOS Safari is unsupported.
⚠️ Controls drive a real heating element. They stay locked until you enable them. Keep the fan on, don't leave it unattended, use HEATER OFF if anything looks wrong.

Auto fan holds your start speed early, eases down only slightly until the bean hits 160 °C, then drops faster to a floor 30% below your start (e.g. 60 → 42) by the end of the profile. It follows the measured bean temp, and overrides the per-point fan column while running.
Build a target-temperature curve: time from Charge → target °C, with optional fan %. When you Run it, the app ramps the PID setpoint smoothly along the curve (leave fan blank to control the fan by hand). Saved in this browser.
