Web-Bluetooth controller for Hamid / Matchbox (TC4) roasters — no vendor app, no WeChat.
Web Bluetooth isn't available. Use Chrome or Edge on Android or Windows/macOS/Linux, Bluetooth on. iOS Safari is unsupported.
⚠️ Controls drive a real heating element. They stay locked until you enable them. Keep the fan on, don't leave it unattended, use HEATER OFF if anything looks wrong.
